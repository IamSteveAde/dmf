Empowering Africa, Inspiring the World

The Dozy Mmobuosi Foundation is a global philanthropic organization committed to advancing human development, social progress, and sustainable solutions across Africa and beyond. Founded by entrepreneur and innovator Dozy Mmobuosi, the Foundation works to break down barriers, unlock opportunities, and create lasting impact in healthcare, education, technology, and community empowerment.
