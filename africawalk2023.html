AFRICA WALK 2023 SPEAKERS

Marlon Nichols
Managing Partner, MaC VC
MaC VC is a $110M early-stage fund backing tech shaped by cultural shifts, with investments in Africa's Sote and Antara Health; co-founder Marlon Nichols has boosted minority representation by 175% at Kauffman Fellows and advises Launch with GS and PledgeLA.
Tom Plofchan
Chief Investment Officer, Pangiam
Pangiam is a tech company shaping the future of secure global travel, with Chief Investment Officer Tom Plofchan leading M&A and growth; formerly a national security advisor, he’s served key roles at U.S. Customs, DHS, and the Department of Energy.
Eric Taylor
Founder & CEO, Trident
Trident is a private equity firm using data-driven strategies to invest in U.S. small businesses in industrial, consumer, and healthcare sectors; founder Eric Taylor, a Harvard grad and CFA charterholder, launched Trident after roles at Goldman Sachs and Brightwood Capital.
Ismail AlHarthi
CEO, Oman Technology Fund
Oman Technology Fund (OTF) aims to position Oman as a tech leader in the Middle East by investing in innovative startups, with Ismail AlHarthi leading global VC growth efforts and serving as Managing Director of Jaoor Ventures, OTF’s growth-stage arm.
Stephanie Busari
Journalist
Stephanie Busari is CNN’s Nigeria bureau chief and digital lead for CNN Africa, known for securing the Chibok girls’ “proof of life” video that led to over 100 rescues.
Kim Oprah
Media Personality & Entrepreneur
Kim Oprah is a dynamic media personality and sports marketing executive, known for her work with Global B and collaborations with the NFF and NWFL; her background in broadcasting, entrepreneurship, and leadership makes her a standout force in entertainment and sports.
Travis Holoway
CEO/Co-founder, SoLoFunds
Travis Holoway is the Co-Founder and CEO of SoLoFunds, a fintech startup recognized by Forbes and CNBC; formerly a financial advisor at Northwestern Mutual, he’s earned honors like Startup of the Year and Inclusive Fintech 50.
Rodney Williams
Co-founder, SoLoFunds
Rodney Williams is the Co-founder and president of SoLo, the leading financial service company for everyday Americans, enabling community banking and lending solutions.
Guy Kamgaing
CEO, StarNews
Guy Kamgaing is the Founder and CEO of StarNews Mobile, revolutionizing Africa’s mobile video market with over 4 million users. A telecom expert with 20 years in Africa, the Middle East, and Asia, he previously founded Globaltech Ventures, supplying network equipment to mobile operators.
Stephenie Coker Rank
Life &Career Transitions Coach
Stephenie Coker Rank is an influential global entrepreneur, seasoned investor, former performing artist and trusted executive search partner, renowned for her empathy and adaptability.
Bibi Kassembe
Founder, FMR Studio
Bibi Kassembe, a luxury branding expert, has worked with brands like Gucci, Louboutin, and Dior before founding FMR Studio in California, a marketing agency specializing in luxury branding. She aims to expand her agency across Africa, Europe, and America, providing top-tier exposure and campaigns for high-end brands.
Chef Obehi El Herf
Founder, ONA
Chef Obehi, is a seasoned international chef, well-versed in the art of preparing various delicacies from different countries across the globe.
Lehlé Balde
Financial Inclusion Advocate
Lehlé Baldé is an award-winning global citizen actively contributing to Agendas 2030 and 2063. She is an International Monetary Fund Youth Fellow and an Acumen fellow, a group of impact-driven leaders tackling the world’s most significant problems.
Kunle Remi
Actor, Filmmaker and Producer
Actor, Host, Model, Filmmaker, and Producer. Hailing from the vibrant Nigerian cities of Lagos and Ibadan, Kunle's artistic journey has taken him across continents.
Sophie Nzinga
Fashion Designer
Sophie Nzinga, a fashion designer and founder of Sophie Zinga, was recognized by Vogue Italia in 2015 and has dressed celebrities like Lupita Nyong'o; she also launched BAAX, a fashion incubator, and The Dakar Design Hub to support young African designers.
Juliet Ibrahim
Actress, Author, Director &Producer
Juliet Ibrahim is a Bilingual multiple award-winning Actress, Author, Speaker, Director/Producer, Influencer, Compere, Philanthropist, and Model who speaks fluent French and English
Kelechi Okoro
Founder, Papaya Studios
Kelechi Okoro is the founder of Papaya Studios and a Director at AFIG Funds, serving on the boards of five African companies. Papaya Studios aims to produce high-quality, authentically African stories, focusing on Nollywood and plans to produce at least 10 premium films annually, contributing to the growth of the African film industry.
Obafemi Banigbe
CEO, TV Everywhere
Obafemi Banigbe is a seasoned technology leader, entrepreneur, advisor, and thought-leader, who has been at the driver’s seat of technology innovations and initiatives across key markets in Africa, as a C-Level executive.
Toke Makinwa
Media Personality & Entrepreneur
Toke Makinwa i s a multi- talented Nigerian personality known for her roles as a radio host, television presenter, vlogger, lifestyle entrepreneur, actress, and author.
Teni
Artiste
Teniola Apata, professionally known as "Teni The Entertainer," is a Nigerian singer, songwriter, and entertainer. She began her music career by releasing her debut single "Amen" while being signed to Shizzi's Magic Fingers Records.
Muriel Lamin
Board Member, Africa Centre
Muriel is a commercial and business strategist with extensive experience across the global media landscape, She leads a multifunctional team workingacrossphilanthropicpartnerships, business development and strategy, technical operations, and communications.
D‘banj
Artiste &Entrepreneur
D'banj (real name: Oladapo Daniel Oyebanjo) is a Nigerian singer, songwriter, and entrepreneur, who is widely recognized as one of the pioneers of the Afrobeatgenre.
ID Cabasa
Music Producer
ID Cabasa, whose real name is Olumide Ogunade, is a Nigerian music producer and songwriter. He is a prominent figure in the Nigerian music industry and is considered one of the pioneers of modern Nigerian music production.
Adebola Williams
Chairman AW Networks
Adebola Williams is the Chairman, AW Network, a Technology and Media holding company with investments in RED, The Future Awards Africa, and Y!/ YNaija.com. Williams is an Entrepreneur, Investor, Storyteller, Political Consultant and strategic advisor to CEO’s and HNI’s.
9ice
Artiste
9ice, Nigerian musician and CEO of Alapomeji Ancestral Records, is known for incorporating Yoruba language into his music. His hit "Gongo Aso" earned multiple awards, including Best Hip Hop Artist at the MTV Africa Music Awards.
Mike Ezuruonye
Actor & Director
Mike Ezuruonye is a renowned figure in the Nigerian movie industry, Nollywood. With a career spanning over 16 years, hehas madea name for himself as an actor, producer, director, model, entrepreneur, andtelevision personality. Before venturing into acting, heworkedinthebankingsector
Lola Pedro
Co-Founder at Pedro's
Lola Pedro, a graduate of University College London, has diverse experience spanning research, tech, marketing, and media. Previously the regional director for Africa at TrendWatching, she's now co-founder of Pedro's ogogoro, a premium organic palm spirit brand in Nigeria, pioneering the country's first indigenous craft spirit category.
Yetunde Aina
CEO, JadeasTrust
Yetunde Aina is CEO and Creative Director of Jadeas Trust, an Educational and Cultural foundation with a Global Africa focus and many years of providing impactful business support to creativeandculturalindustries
Kunle Soriyan
Futurist & Thought Leader
Kunle Soriyan is a Nigerian motivational speaker, life coach, and leadership expert. He is known for his inspiring talks and workshops that aim to empower individuals and organizations to reach their full potential.
Segun Cole
Entrepreneur
Segun Cole is a Serial entrepreneur; he started his Career as a New mobility Consultant advising Governments on micro mobility policies. He is an Investor and also sits on the Board of various African Tech Startups.
Riyah Abdul
Entrepreneur

Kiswana Browne
Senior Investment Associate
Kiswana Browne is a senior associate at Newark Venture Partners. Previously a venture capital investor at Plug and Play Ventures, she led investments in fintech, digital health, and enterprise tech. Kiswana also founded Digivise, a social impact tech company, and holds an MBA from The University of Chicago Booth.
Peace Hyde
Television Producer
