About Us

The Dozy Mmobuosi Foundation is a global philanthropic organisation dedicated to advancing human development, driving social progress, and creating sustainable solutions across Africa and beyond. Established with a vision to break barriers and open new pathways of opportunity, the Foundation invests in critical sectors including healthcare, education, technology, and community empowerment. Our work is rooted in the belief that every individual deserves the chance to thrive, and through our programmes, partnerships, and interventions, we are building systems of support that foster resilience, dignity, and long-term impact.
