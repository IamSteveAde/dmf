Unicorn Group

Building the Future of African Innovation

At Unicorn Group, we are solving Africa’s most pressing challenges faced by tech and innovation-driven businesses. From incubation to execution, scaling to market integration — through a world-class ecosystem approach and patient, value-aligned capital.

The Market Challenges

Incubation

Inadequate infrastructure, limited mentorship, and lack of enabling environments.

Execution

Operational know-how and support often lacking during execution stages.

Scale

Challenges transitioning from proof-of-concept to scalable businesses.

Fragmented Markets

Multiple currencies, regulations, and systems across 54 countries.

The Market Opportunity

Across Africa, startups and tech businesses face fragmented markets, under-developed infrastructure, and lack of access to capital and support. Unicorn Group is uniquely positioned to provide the support needed to build the next generation of African champions by combining local understanding with global best practices.

01. Incubation Support

Curated innovation hubs and incubation spaces across Africa, offering tailored startup programs and world-class mentorship ecosystems.

02.Execution Enablement

Dedicated operating partners, tools, and expert resources guiding businesses through operations, finance, legal, branding, and HR.

03. Scaling Pathways

Strategic global partnerships and access to networks that help African ventures grow beyond proof of concept to global markets.

04. Market Integration

Cross-border unification strategies to overcome Africa’s fragmented markets, backed by long-term patient capital.

Our approach to addressing these challenges

Accelerators & Incubators

We have partnerships with a global network of incubators and accelerators

Structured Programs

Equipping startups with essential tools for early and growth-stage success.

Innovation Campuses

State-of-the-art spaces that foster collaboration and scale.

Resident Partners

Operational experts who provide hands-on business guidance.

Advisory Services

Legal, finance, HR, ESG, branding, and marketing advisory clinics.

Global Partnerships

Linkages to global experts, institutions, and talent.
