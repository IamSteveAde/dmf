Our Commitment

Healthcare for All

Committed to ensuring equitable access to quality healthcare for every individual, the Foundation seeks to break down barriers to medical care and provide essential services to communities across Nigeria and beyond. Our approach combines innovation, outreach, and community engagement to deliver meaningful, lasting health improvements.

The Dozy Mmobuosi Foundation has launched a $10 million mobile prostate cancer screening initiative and established state-of-the-art diagnostic clinics in Lekki, equipped with advanced medical equipment and modern healthcare technologies. These initiatives provide early detection, preventive care, and continuous support to patients, particularly men and women who may not have access to timely medical attention. Beyond clinical interventions, the Foundation organizes comprehensive community health education programs, workshops, and awareness campaigns to teach preventive measures, promote healthy lifestyles, and address pressing public health challenges. Our outreach extends to vaccination drives, maternal and child health programs, and targeted initiatives for chronic disease management, ensuring that underserved populations receive consistent and reliable care. Through partnerships with local hospitals, healthcare professionals, and global organizations, we strengthen the healthcare infrastructure and train medical personnel to deliver quality care. By integrating cutting-edge technology, compassionate services, and community-driven solutions, the Foundation not only improves immediate health outcomes but also contributes to building resilient health systems capable of sustaining long-term impact.

Shaping Futures

Education & Innovation

Empowering the next generation through scholarships, grants, and transformative educational programs.

The Foundation provides scholarships, supports innovative learning initiatives, and has pioneered a student loan program with deferred repayment options. These programs are designed to remove financial barriers, promote academic excellence, and equip young Africans with the skills, knowledge, and entrepreneurial mindset needed to thrive in a rapidly evolving world.

Technology & Empowerment

Equipping young Africans with digital skills, innovation opportunities, and tools to thrive in a tech-driven world.

The Foundation supports programs that foster technology skills, digital literacy, and innovation across sectors including healthcare, fintech, and community services. By providing access to mentorship, workshops, and cutting-edge tools, we enable youth to develop solutions that address local challenges and drive sustainable growth.

Community Development

Fostering inclusive growth and stronger communities through sustainable infrastructure, social programs, and support initiatives.

The Foundation has delivered mobile cancer screenings, organized medical outreach programs, and provided grants to support widows, smallholder farmers, and underserved communities. By addressing local challenges and empowering residents, we aim to build resilient communities where everyone has the opportunity to thrive.
